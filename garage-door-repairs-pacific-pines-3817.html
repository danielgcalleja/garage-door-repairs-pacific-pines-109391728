How to Extend the Longevity of Your Garage Door

Increasing the lifespan of your garage door is essential for maintaining the efficiency and look. Regular maintenance is vital to ensuring that your garage door functions without issues. Lubricating the moving parts including hinges, rollers, and tracks is necessary every few months to avoid wear and tear. Additionally, regularly checking for damaged hardware can assist in preventing any potential problems down the road.

Another effective way to increase the lifespan of your garage door involves ensuring it is adequately aligned. Misalignment can lead to uneven stress on the door and its components, ultimately resulting in malfunctions. Reviewing the door's alignment semi-annually can spare you from costly repairs. Lastly, keeping the area around the garage door free of debris and clutter can in addition contribute to a longer-lasting door, as it avoids any accidental damage during use.
