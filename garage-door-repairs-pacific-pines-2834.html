How to Increase the Longevity of Your Garage Door

Extending the longevity of your garage door is essential for maintaining the efficiency and aesthetics. Regular care is vital to ensuring that your garage door operates smoothly. Oiling the moving parts like hinges, rollers, and tracks is necessary every few months to prevent wear and tear. Additionally, regularly checking for worn hardware can assist in staving off any potential problems down the road.

Another beneficial way to increase the longevity of your garage door is ensuring it is correctly aligned. Misalignment can lead to uneven stress on the door and its components, ultimately resulting in malfunctions. Looking over the door's alignment every six months can spare you from costly repairs. Lastly, keeping the area around the garage door clear of debris and items can in addition contribute to a longer-lasting door, as it stops any accidental damage during use.
