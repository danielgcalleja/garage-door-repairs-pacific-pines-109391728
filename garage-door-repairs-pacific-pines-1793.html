How to Increase the Longevity of Your Garage Door

Increasing the lifespan of your garage door matters greatly for maintaining the performance and look. Regular upkeep is vital to ensuring that your garage door operates without issues. Oiling the moving parts such as hinges, rollers, and tracks is necessary every few months to reduce wear and tear. Additionally, regularly checking for loose hardware can aid in avoiding any potential problems down the road.

Another beneficial way to extend the lifespan of your garage door involves ensuring it is properly aligned. Misalignment can lead to premature wear on the door and its components, ultimately resulting in malfunctions. Inspecting the door's alignment semi-annually can spare you from costly repairs. Lastly, keeping the area around the garage door unobstructed of debris and items can also contribute to a longer-lasting door, as it avoids any accidental damage during use.
